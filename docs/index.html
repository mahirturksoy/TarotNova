❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my Premium subscription?

Go to Settings → Apple ID → Subscriptions → TarotNova → Cancel Subscription. Your access continues until the end of your billing period.

How do I restore my purchases?

Open the app → Go to the Premium screen → Tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.

How do I delete my account and data?

To delete your account and all associated data, please send an email to turksoymahir@gmail.com with the subject "Account Deletion Request". We will process your request within 7 business days and confirm once completed.

What is TarotNova Premium?

TarotNova Premium unlocks exclusive tarot spreads, unlimited AI-powered readings, and advanced features. You can subscribe monthly or annually through the app.

Are the tarot readings accurate?

TarotNova uses advanced AI to generate personalized interpretations based on your question, mood, and selected cards. Readings are for entertainment and self-reflection purposes only and should not replace professional advice.

The app isn't working properly. What should I do?

Please try the following: 1) Close and reopen the app, 2) Check your internet connection, 3) Update to the latest version from the App Store, 4) Restart your device. If the issue persists, contact us at turksoymahir@gmail.com with a description of the problem.
